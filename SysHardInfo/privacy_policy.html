SysHard Info Privacy Policy

About Us

This is our privacy policy regarding to the SysHard Info app. This app does not collect any user's personal information in any manner.

Major permissions

Camera

SysHard Info requires this permission to show camera information like picture resolution. SysHard Info does not take any photos or videos.

Storage

SysHard Info requires this permission to detect external storage (SD-card) total and free space. Also this permission is used for saving the detailed report to external storage.

Location

This permission is required for showing Wi-Fi frequency information in the Network Tab.

Phone

This permission is required for showing the current data connection state (cellular) in the Network Tab.
